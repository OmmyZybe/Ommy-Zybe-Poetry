Whispers of the Wind

The wind carries whispers I can barely hear,
Echoes of silence drawing me near,
In each breeze a secret, soft and thin,
A message from the world within.

Midnight Ink

Under stars, my pen bleeds thought,
Lines of love and battles fought,
Pages cry with every stroke,
Midnight ink, the voice I spoke.
